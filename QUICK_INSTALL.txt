RTW2 RIVAL EMPIRES 0.9.1 — QUICK INSTALL

1. Extract rtw2_rival_empires_v0.9.1.zip.
2. Close Total War: ROME II.
3. Copy @rtw2_rival_empires_balanced.pack to the Rome II data folder.
4. Open the launcher and Mod Manager.
5. Enable "Run user script mods".
6. Enable the Rival Empires pack.
7. Load a single-player Grand Campaign save.
8. Advance through the next faction turns so the AI bundles refresh.

Use the standalone balanced pack for normal installation.

Do not use compatibility/@rtw2_rival_empires_balanced_module_only.pack unless
you have manually merged Rival Empires into another all_scripted.lua loader.

At maximum human Imperium, full-tier rivals receive +42% research, -36%
building cost, -1 construction turn for verified core building sets, and +4
provincial public order. Established non-champion rivals receive one tier less.

Current human allies, clients, vassals, satrapies, rebels, and civil-war
factions are excluded.
